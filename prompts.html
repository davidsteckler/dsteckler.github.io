🤖 Weird Ways to Use AI

Most people use AI assistants like a tool. Here's how to use them like a creative partner with an alien brain.

🔍 How to Prompt AI

🎯 Objective 📝 Prompt Example 💡 Tips
Be Clear & Specific "Generate a 300-word essay on the impact of AI in education." Clearly define the scope and length to guide the AI effectively.
Provide Context "As a project manager, outline a plan to integrate AI tools into our workflow." Giving context helps the AI tailor responses to your specific role or scenario.
Set Constraints "List five unconventional ways to use AI without relying on traditional applications." Constraints drive creativity by pushing the AI to think outside the box.
Ask Open-Ended Questions "How can AI transform personal development strategies?" Open-ended questions encourage comprehensive and exploratory answers.
Use Step-by-Step Instructions "Explain how to create a digital doppelgänger using AI in three steps." Breaking down tasks helps the AI provide structured and actionable guidance.
Encourage Creativity "Imagine AI as a silent partner in your daily routine. How would it enhance your productivity?" Encouraging creative thinking can lead to innovative and unexpected solutions.
Request Analogies "Compare the relationship between a user and AI to that of a gardener and their plants." Analogies can simplify complex concepts and make the interaction more relatable.
Incorporate Emotions "Describe how using AI to write your apology letter might make you feel more genuine." Integrating emotions can add depth and authenticity to the AI's responses.
Specify Format "Create a bullet-point list of 10 ways AI can assist in strategic planning." Specifying the desired format ensures the output aligns with your presentation needs.
Seek Alternatives "Provide alternative methods to traditional AI applications in business management." Asking for alternatives fosters innovative thinking and diversifies AI usage scenarios.
