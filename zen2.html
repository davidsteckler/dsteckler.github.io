What this is

One passage per day, drawn mostly from Stoic and contemplative writers who wrote about the gap between sensation and the story we tell about it. New passage at midnight. The same passage all day, even if you reload.

Each passage has a small note translating it for an anxious body — for those of us who notice our heart and our breath and slide into alarm.

The "right now" button is for active spikes. It pulls attention outward through the senses, away from the body. No breath instructions, no "notice the sensation." The opposite move.

The log is private to your browser. It never leaves your device. Its purpose is not insight — it's quiet evidence, accumulated over weeks, that the spikes always pass.

This is a contemplative tool, not therapy or medical care. If panic is shaping your life, CBT with someone who specializes in panic disorder is the highest-leverage thing you can do. This page is a small companion, nothing more.
