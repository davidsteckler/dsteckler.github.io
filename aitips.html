Learning Notes
Watch the tutorial to see the stroke order, then copy it.
Lift your pen freely — dots, crossbars, and tails are all separate strokes.
Hit "Done" when you've finished all strokes to get scored.
Letters like i j t f x always need 2+ strokes.
Undo last stroke If you mess up one stroke, undo just that one and redo it without clearing everything.
