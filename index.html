Me in 10 seconds

7th grade computer science educator. Math origin. Embracer of challenges. Philosopher in the classroom. Game strategist. Lifelong learner. Seeker of difficult paths. Problem-solver. Curiosity-driven. Always open to new perspectives.

About Me

I teach 7th grade computer science, blending math, robotics, and philosophy. No CS degree—just a passion for turning puzzles into lessons. Inspired by ethical dilemmas I once studied, I challenge students to learn at their own pace—there’s no speed limit to knowledge.

What I’m Doing Now

Though in my 9th year at Highlands, I’m as curious as day one. Fresh from Puerto Vallarta, I’m designing lessons that merge travel stories, code, and Pixie-powered breaks with my dog.
