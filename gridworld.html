Understanding the Grid

Tracy lives in a grid world that is 400 pixels wide x 400 pixels tall. Tracy always starts at the center of the canvas, at point (0,0). Click anywhere on the grid to move Tracy!

Key Concepts:

  • The grid is 400x400 pixels, with each grid box being 20x20 pixels
  • The center (0,0) is in the middle of the grid
  • X-coordinates: Positive to the right, negative to the left
  • Y-coordinates: Positive upwards, negative downwards
  • Tracy can move beyond the visible grid (-200 to 200 for both x and y)
  • Each grid line represents a change of 20 in either x or y coordinate
  • You can use the command goto(x,y) to move Tracy to specific coordinates
