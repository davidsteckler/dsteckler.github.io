Introduction to Basic Drawing Commands

Welcome to Tracy's Drawing Adventure Quiz! Here, you'll learn and test your understanding of basic drawing commands used to control Tracy's movements and drawings.

Master these commands to create intricate drawings and ensure your map is accurate.

  • penup(): Lifts Tracy's pen, allowing her to move without drawing. Think "Sky writing!"
  • pendown(): Lowers Tracy's pen, enabling her to draw while moving. Think "Ground painting!"
  • forward(distance): Moves Tracy forward by the specified distance. Think "Tracy's dance steps!"
  • backward(distance): Moves Tracy backward by the specified distance.

Understanding how these commands interact is key to creating seamless and accurate drawings.
