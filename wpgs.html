AI Usage Continuum for Students

White Plains City School District

"To educate and inspire all students, while nurturing their dreams, so they learn continually, think critically, pursue their aspirations and contribute to a diverse and dynamic world."

Level Description Student Usage Mission Alignment
Level 0: No AI 		Traditional learning methods without AI tools
  • Manual research and note-taking
  • Handwritten assignments and projects
  • In-person discussions and collaborations
 Builds foundational skills and encourages independent problem-solving
Level 1: AI Assisted 		Use of AI tools for specific tasks and support
  • AI-powered spell and grammar checkers
  • Educational apps that provide instant feedback
  • AI-based flashcard generators for study
 Enhances learning efficiency and supports continuous improvement
Level 2: AI Enhanced 		Comprehensive use of AI tools integrated into learning processes
  • AI-driven personalized study plans
  • Virtual tutors for subject-specific assistance
  • Collaborative AI tools for group projects
 Promotes personalized learning and collaborative innovation
Level 3: AI Integrated Goal The full integration of AI allows for the creation of things that were previously impossible.
  • Empowering students as critical thinkers, creators, and problem solvers.
  • Students are responsible for human oversight and evaluation of all AI-generated content.
  • All AI tools used and how they were used should be cited.
 Ensures sustainable and ethical AI usage, fostering a culture of accountability and innovation.

Empowering Our Mission with AI

As students progress through these levels, they continuously align with our mission by:

  • Fostering critical thinking at every stage of AI integration
  • Inspiring students to explore AI's potential in various fields
  • Nurturing dreams by showcasing AI's role in future careers
  • Preparing students to contribute meaningfully to our tech-driven world
