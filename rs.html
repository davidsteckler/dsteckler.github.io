Tracy's World - Remixed Slinky Project

Create a unique slinky design using Tracy's circle and forward commands. Your task: craft a patternA repeated or recurring sequence in your design that fits within Tracy's 400x400 grid.

Project Requirements:

  • Use at least 6 circle commands
  • Use at least 3 different circle sizes
  • Use at least 5 forward commands
  • Use at least 2 different forward distances
  • Keep your design within the 400x400 grid
  • Create a clear rhythmA regular, repeated pattern of shapes or movements in your design in your design
