Made a GitHub account, messed around on Trinket.io with HTML. Ran ChatGPT's designs through the wringer about five or six times.

Back to GitHub, made a repository, linked my domain. Boom! Site was live. Well, sort of. Buttons didn't work. Classic.

Couple hours of ChatGPT chats, screenshots, and "What now?" moments later, I had working buttons and a newsletter signup.
