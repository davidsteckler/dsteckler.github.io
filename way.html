How to Play
The Goal
Move your traveler across the hex map from Start to the glowing Destination. The world is bigger than your resources — plan your route carefully.
Resources
Food is consumed crossing forests and plains. Water drains in deserts and mountains. Stamina depletes on every move. If any resource hits zero, you collapse.
Terrain
Plains are cheap. Forests cost food. Mountains drain water and stamina. Deserts punish hard. Rivers restore water. Villages restore all. Ruins and caves trigger events. Shrines grant blessings.
Events
Special hexes trigger encounters — strangers, discoveries, dangers. Each choice has real consequences. Read carefully before you commit.
Seasons
The world changes as you travel. Summer dries rivers. Winter freezes passes. Spring floods plains. Each run starts in a random season.
The Hook
The map generates fresh every run. Your class changes which terrain is dangerous. The destination moves. You will find a better route the second time. And a worse one.
