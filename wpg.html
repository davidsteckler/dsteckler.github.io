AI Usage Continuum for Teachers

White Plains City School District

"To educate and inspire all students, while nurturing their dreams, so they learn continually, think critically, pursue their aspirations and contribute to a diverse and dynamic world."

Level Description Teaching Approach Mission Alignment
Level 0: No AI 		Traditional teaching methods without AI assistance
  • Focus on core skills
  • Direct instruction
  • Hands-on activities
 Emphasizes critical thinking and independent learning
Level 1: AI Assisted 		Limited AI use for specific tasks
  • AI-enhanced lesson planning
  • Automated grading tools
  • AI-generated practice problems
 Supports continuous learning and inspiration through innovative methods
Level 2: AI Enhanced 		Full integration of AI throughout the teaching process
  • Personalized learning paths
  • AI-driven project suggestions
  • Real-time feedback systems
 Prepares students to contribute to a diverse and dynamic world through technology
Level 3: AI Integrated Goal The full integration of AI allows for the creation of things that were previously impossible.
  • Empowering students as critical thinkers, creators, and problem solvers.
  • Student is responsible for human oversight and evaluation of all AI generated content.
  • All AI tools used and how they were used should be cited.
 Ensures sustainable and ethical AI usage, fostering a culture of accountability and innovation.

Empowering Our Mission with AI

As we progress through these levels, we continuously align with our mission by:

  • Fostering critical thinking at every stage of AI integration
  • Inspiring students to explore AI's potential in various fields
  • Nurturing dreams by showcasing AI's role in future careers
  • Preparing students to contribute meaningfully to our tech-driven world
